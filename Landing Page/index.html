A landing page is a standalone web page that users arrive at after clicking on a link or ad from a search engine, social media, or email. Landing pages are a key marketing tool that's designed to encourage users to take a specific action, such as making a purchase, subscribing to a newsletter, or downloading a guide. Landing pages are different from other web pages because they're not part of a website's navigation and are instead used for a specific purpose within an advertising campaign. When done correctly, landing pages can help reduce customer acquisition costs and drive conversions.
